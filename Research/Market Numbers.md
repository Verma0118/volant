## Competitor Pricing (verified Apr 2026)

### Samsara (trucking — our core analogy)
- **Actual price:** $27–33/vehicle/month (software + hardware bundle)
- **Source:** Expert Market review, Sep 2025; ITQlick Oct 2024
- **Confidence:** High
- **Note:** Previous deck said "$300-600/vehicle/YEAR" — that was WRONG. Correct figure is $27-33/MONTH. Our $200-500/aircraft/month hypothesis is a 6-15x premium over Samsara. Requires explicit justification.

### FlytBase (drone dock management)
- **Free tier:** $0 (Zero plan — basic dock ops)
- **Pro:** $99/month per org (NOT per aircraft)
- **Enterprise:** Custom ("Let's talk")
- **Source:** FlytBase pricing page, live Apr 2026
- **Confidence:** High
- **Critical insight:** FlytBase is dock/automation management — hardware-centric, DJI dock focused. NOT a fleet ops management platform. LESS competitive overlap with Volant than feared.

### DroneDeploy (drone mapping/photogrammetry)
- **Individual:** $329-349/month per user/org
- **Advanced:** $499-649/month per user/org
- **Enterprise:** Custom
- **Source:** DroneDeploy pricing page + thefuture3d.com review, 2026
- **Confidence:** High
- **Critical insight:** DroneDeploy is a MAPPING platform (orthomosaics, 3D models, construction progress). NOT fleet ops. Users fly drones to capture data, not manage fleets. DIFFERENT product category from Volant.

### Competitive Landscape Revised
After actual pricing research — the "competitive" landscape is less crowded than the council assumed:
- **FlytBase:** dock automation ≠ fleet ops management
- **DroneDeploy:** mapping/photogrammetry ≠ fleet ops management
- **Airdata UAV:** flight log analytics ≠ fleet ops management
- **Actual gap:** No direct competitor offering unified real-time fleet visibility + mission dispatch + maintenance tracking + FAA compliance in one ops platform

## Pricing Hypothesis — Revised

### Our Hypothesis: $200–500/aircraft/month
- **Status:** Unvalidated. Still a hypothesis.
- **Samsara comparable (corrected):** $27-33/vehicle/month for trucking
- **Our premium:** 6-15x Samsara
- **Justification required:** Aviation regulatory complexity (FAA Part 107/135 compliance, LAANC, airspace coordination), safety-critical operations, expensive aircraft assets
- **Credible range:** $150-300/aircraft/month (4-9x Samsara) is more defensible
- **$500/aircraft/month:** Possible for eVTOL operators managing $3M+ aircraft, but aggressive for drone operators
- **Recommendation:** Segment pricing — drone operators: $150-250/aircraft/month; eVTOL operators: $300-500/aircraft/month

### Unit Economics Check (revised)
| Customer type | Fleet size | Monthly price | MRR | ARR |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Drone inspection co | 20 aircraft | $200/aircraft | $4,000 | $48,000 |
| Drone delivery co | 50 aircraft | $200/aircraft | $10,000 | $120,000 |
| eVTOL operator (future) | 30 aircraft | $400/aircraft | $12,000 | $144,000 |

- **LTV/CAC target:** >3x (B2B SaaS benchmark median)
- **Gross margin target:** 70-80% (SaaS standard)
- **CAC payback:** 12-18 months for SMB drone operators with founder-led sales
