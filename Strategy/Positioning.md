# Positioning

## Core Analogy
"What Samsara built for trucking, we're building for autonomous aviation."

Use this as the lead. Samsara is a ~$17B company (Apr 2026). Anyone in ops software knows it.

## Why This Works
- Samsara = fleet telematics, route optimization, compliance, driver behavior, maintenance scheduling
- We = same problem set but for aircraft: flight ops, battery/charge management, maintenance cycles, regulatory compliance (FAA), pilot/operator dispatch
- The analogy is immediately legible to operators, investors, and industry contacts

## Target Customer (initial)
**Drone fleet operators** (bridge market):
- Commercial inspection companies (infrastructure, energy, construction)
- Delivery drone operators (Wing, Zipline partners, regional players)
- Enterprise drone programs (oil & gas, agriculture, logistics)

**Future: eVTOL operators** (primary market):
- Air taxi networks (Archer, Joby, Lilium successors)
- Regional air mobility operators
- Cargo eVTOL (Wisk, Beta Technologies)

## Positioning Statement
For drone and eVTOL fleet operators who manage multiple aircraft across complex operations, Volant is the fleet operations platform that gives operators real-time fleet visibility, mission dispatch, maintenance tracking, FAA compliance logging, and utilization analytics — purpose-built for aviation, not adapted from trucking software.

## What the MVP Delivers (5 capabilities)
1. **Real-time fleet visibility** — live map + status table, every aircraft in one view
2. **Mission dispatch** — aircraft assignment, deconfliction, route validation
3. **Maintenance tracking** — battery cycles, flight hours, work orders, failure prediction
4. **FAA compliance logging** — Part 107/135 records auto-generated, LAANC in ~1.4s
5. **Utilization analytics** — cost per flight hour, downtime rates, fleet profitability
